A speculative research note about an intermediate regime between local and global credal compression.

<!--more-->



The possible phenomenon is that finite observation windows induce a genuinely regularized rate:

$$
R_k(D) \neq R_1(D),
\qquad
R_k(D) \neq R_\infty(D).
$$

The research question is whether there exists a sharp critical window

$$
k_{\min}(\varepsilon)
$$

separating the finite-window regime from the global regime.
