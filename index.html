Hello, I'm Nicolò Bonacorsi

I am an M.S. student and researcher in Applied Mathematics at Columbia University . My research interests lie at the intersection of stochastic control , optimal stopping , stochastic differential equations , uncertainty quantification , imprecise probability , information theory , and mathematical finance .

I am currently working with Professor Ioannis Karatzas at Columbia University on stochastic control and optimal stopping, and with Professor Francesco Restuccia at Northeastern University on credal uncertainty, robust information-theoretic models, and uncertainty-aware compression. My long-term goal is to develop rigorous mathematical tools for decision-making under uncertainty, with applications to quantitative research and financial modeling.
