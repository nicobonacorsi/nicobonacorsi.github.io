Hello, I'm Nicolò Bonacorsi

I am an M.S. student and researcher in Applied Mathematics at Columbia University. My research interests include stochastic control, optimal stopping, Hamilton–Jacobi–Bellman equations, imprecise probabilities, uncertainty quantification, credal uncertainty, information theory, robust probabilistic compression, and mathematical finance.

I am currently working with Professor Ioannis Karatzas at Columbia University on stochastic control and optimal stopping, and with Professor Francesco Restuccia at Northeastern University on credal uncertainty, robust information-theoretic models, and uncertainty-aware compression.
